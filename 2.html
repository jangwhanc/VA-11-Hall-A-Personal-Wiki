Gillian

Gillian is a bartender at VA-11 Hall-A with a mysterious past. He is known to occasionally disappear for a few days, but is otherwise a dependable employee of VA-11 Hall-A. Has some connection to Jamie, a professional assassin. Officially he is known as "Robert", his predecessor at VA-11 Hall-A, whose name he took to disguise his true identity. He also hates bell peppers, even when it is mixed in with food as small bits. He lives in an apartment nearby VA-11 Hall-A Dana managed to get thanks to the BTC housing program
